About Us

Khidmat Foundation, established in 2019, is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving humanity with compassion and equality. Since its inception, the foundation has been actively engaged in social welfare activities, focusing on feeding the hungry, supporting education, providing medical aid, and empowering underprivileged communities. Over the years, Khidmat Foundation has organized numerous social welfare programs. Along with welfare work, the foundation also celebrates important cultural and social occasions to not only honor great leaders but also to spread their teachings of justice, equality, and social unity. Guided by the spirit of “Khidmat” (service), the foundation continues to work tirelessly to uplift marginalized communities, promote social harmony, and create opportunities for a better future. With the help of volunteers and well-wishers, Khidmat Foundation is striving to build a society where no one is left behind.
